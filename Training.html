Beginner/ 8 hours length

    Canadian Firearms Safety Course (CFSC)

    This course covers these topics:

  • Evolution of firearms, major parts, types and actions
  • Basic firearms safety practices
  • Ammunition
  • Operating firearm actions
  • Safe handling and carry procedures
  • Firing techniques and procedures
  • Care of non-restricted firearms
  • Responsibilities of the firearms owner/user
  • Safe storage, display, transportation and handling of non-restricted firearms

Intermediate/ 8 hours length

    Canadian Restricted Firearms Safety Course (CRFSC)

    This course covers these topics:

  • Evolution of firearms, major parts, types and actions
  • Basic firearms safety practices
  • Ammunition
  • Operating handgun actions
  • Firing techniques and procedures for handguns
  • Care of restricted firearms
  • Responsibilities of the firearms owner/user
  • Safe storage, display, transportation and handling of restricted firearms

Advanced/ 4 hours length

    Bill 9 Test

    This competency test is mandatory for anyone who'd like to partake in the following activities:

  • Use and or own restricted firearms
  • Want to join a shooting club
  • Renew their membership to a shooting club
  • Maintain a valid membership card

    • Additionally, this test is mandatory for those who have not practiced target shooting for at least one year

    You must also have passed the Canadian Restricted Firearms Safety Course in order to register for this course
